Premium Carriers LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website premiumcarriersllc.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information you provide when visiting our Site or contacting us about our trucking and logistics services.

By using our Site, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions, please contact us using the information at the bottom of this page.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information — name, company name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you fill out a contact or inquiry form on our Site.
  • SMS / text message opt-in information — mobile phone number and consent status when you voluntarily provide your number and agree to receive text message communications from us.
  • Communication records — messages and correspondence you send us via phone, email, or web forms.
  • Automatically collected data — standard server logs, IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URL collected through cookies or similar technologies when you browse our Site.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as Social Security numbers, payment card data, or government-issued identification numbers through this Site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to freight inquiries, quote requests, and general questions about our services.
  • Send operational updates, dispatch notifications, or service-related communications you have requested.
  • Send SMS/text message updates if you have opted in to receive them (see section below).
  • Improve the content and functionality of our Site.
  • Comply with applicable law and protect our legal rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

SMS / Text Message Communications

If you provide your mobile phone number and consent to receive text messages from Premium Carriers LLC, we may send you service-related SMS notifications such as dispatch updates or appointment confirmations.

No mobile opt-in information or text message consent will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Your opt-in to receive SMS messages is voluntary. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message we send, or by contacting us directly at the information below. After opting out, you will not receive further SMS messages from us unless you opt in again.

We do not use your mobile number for any purpose other than the service communications you consented to receive.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

We never share SMS opt-in consent or mobile phone numbers collected for text messaging purposes with any third party or affiliate.

We do not use tracking technologies to build advertising profiles or to serve targeted advertisements.
